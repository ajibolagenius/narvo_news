# Narvo

**News That Speaks To You**  
*Smart, audio-first news summaries for Africa and beyond.*

---

## Overview

Narvo is a mobile-first, voice-first platform that transforms how people in emerging markets—especially Nigeria—consume news and information. Users subscribe to their interests, and Narvo aggregates news from global and hyperlocal sources, summarises content using advanced AI, and reads these summaries aloud in natural, localised voices.

---

## Vision & Mission

**Vision:** To become the go-to platform for simplified, voice-enabled news summaries across Africa and the global south.

**Mission:** Deliver curated, trustworthy, and inclusive news experiences that empower everyday people to stay informed effortlessly.

---

## Key Problems Solved

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Information Overload | AI-powered summaries by user-selected interests |
| Literacy & Language Barriers | Natural voice playback in local dialects |
| Reading Fatigue & Time Constraints | Hands-free audio playback |
| Limited Internet Access | Offline downloads of news summaries |
| Fragmented Local News Sources | Aggregates from web, radio, TV, YouTube, etc. |
| Visual Stress & Accessibility | Screen-free, voice-first experience |
| Cultural Disconnect | Localised voices, topics, and formats |

---

## Core Features

- **Smart Summaries:** AI-curated news using GPT-4, Cohere
- **Voice Playback:** Multilingual narration (ElevenLabs, Google, Coqui TTS)
- **Interest Picker:** Personalized onboarding
- **Offline Support:** Downloadable text/audio summaries
- **Bookmarks & Preferences:** Save and customize habits
- **Topic Grid Selector:** Emoji-rich, pill-designed UI
- **Smart Notifications:** Intelligent alerts
- **Voice Navigation:** Full accessibility support
